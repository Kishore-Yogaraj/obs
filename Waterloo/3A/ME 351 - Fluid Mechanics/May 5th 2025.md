#### Intensive vs Extensive properties
- **Intensive**
	- A property that does not change as the amount of matter changes
	- Temperature, density, pressure, velocity, viscosity
- **Extensive**
	- A property that changes as the amount of matter changes
	- Kinetic energy
	- Momentum
