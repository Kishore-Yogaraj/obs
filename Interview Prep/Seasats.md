
### What is Seasats
Seasats builds and operates high-endurance, user-friendly autonomous surface vehicles that collect data for defense, research and commercial customers. Seasats vehicles carry sensors into places that are either too dangerous or too expensive to reach with crewed vessels which can drastically reduce operational risks and costs.
