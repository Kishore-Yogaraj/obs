**Do not make a single aspect of your identity be an overwhelming portion of who you are.**

The more you let a single belief define you the less capable you are of adapting when life challenges you. If you tie everything up to one thing like being the point guard or making partner at your firm then the loss of that part of your life will wreck you. When you cling too tightly to one identity you become brittle. Lose that one thing and you lose yourself. Your identity should be something flexible rather than brittle.Your identity should work with changing circumstances rather than against them.

- "I'm an athlete" becomes "I'm the type of person who is mentally tough and loves a physical challenge"
- **"I'm the CEO"** becomes "I'm the type of person who builds and creates things."
